About Us

Founded in 2019 here in Agadir, AuraWagen started with a simple idea — luxury is something you should feel, not just see. From the beginning, we wanted to create more than just a car rental or sales service. For us, it’s about the experience, the details, and how every client feels when they choose us. We carefully select every vehicle and do our best to make sure everything goes smoothly, so you can just enjoy your moment without stress. Behind AuraWagen is a small but passionate team, led by two women, Nassima Taleb and Maryam Elkhalki, who built this project step by step with one clear goal: to bring a new level of luxury mobility to Morocco.
